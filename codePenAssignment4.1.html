Controversial Decisions

Lord Commander Snow made several controversial decsions that ultimately led to saving the people of The Seven Kingdoms. Those decisions were:

  • Wildlings through the walls and giving them military positions to fight the White Walkers
  • Working with Stannis Barantheon
  • Sending men to Hardhome
  • Defying Ramsey Bolton and taking inferior numbers and fighters to battle him.
  • Remaining neutral as the Seven Kingdom's Warred
  • Bending the knee to Daenerys Targaryen
